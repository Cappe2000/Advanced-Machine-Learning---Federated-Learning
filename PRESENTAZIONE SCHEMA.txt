1 descrizione PARAMETRI e splitting client descrizione ambiente (fa solo il primo)

cifar 100 data split

fedavg model and fedprox model
How they relate to your project (what you're actually doing)

In your code you intentionally run a hybrid: some selected clients (those for which you reuse the probe model) continue training with plain SGD (FedAvg-style), while others (those that start from the global snapshot) use the FedProx objective.

This hybrid yields two practical outcomes:

Probe-reused clients behave like FedAvg (they send larger, unconstrained deltas — good for fast short-term progress when probes are predictive).

Non-reused clients behave like FedProx (they are regularized toward the global model, reducing drift).

The hybrid is a deliberate trade-off: reuse makes probes predictive and saves compute, while FedProx on other clients stabilizes training for those that would otherwise drift.
